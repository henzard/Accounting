Design guidance

No pure black—always navy-tinted

Gold works best on dark mode (icons, focus rings, key numbers)

Avoid glow effects; rely on contrast and spacing instead

Typography & UI Pairing (Strongly Recommended)

To complete the “expensive” feel:

Headings: Serif or high-contrast sans
Examples: Playfair Display, Canela, Cormorant, or Inter (tight tracking)

Body: Neutral, modern sans
Examples: Inter, Source Sans 3, IBM Plex Sans

Letter spacing: Slightly increased on headings

Font weights: Fewer choices (Regular, Medium, Semibold)

Summary: Visual Personality

Minimalist – few colors, disciplined usage

Elegant – warm gold against disciplined blues

Expensive – no loud accents, no playful saturation

Timeless – avoids trends, suitable for long-lived products
