## 🎯 Brand Identity

### Concept
"Homebase" represents:
- Your household's financial headquarters
- A secure base for financial stability
- The foundation for building wealth
- Home as the center of Dave Ramsey's principles

### Shield Symbol
- **Dollar Sign ($)**: Financial focus
- **Upward Arrow**: Growth, progress, debt payoff
- **Shield**: Protection, security, strength
- **Combination**: Protecting and growing your wealth

---

## 📐 Design Principles

### Visual Style
- **Clean & Modern**: Simple, uncluttered interfaces
- **Professional**: Financial app credibility
- **Approachable**: Not intimidating, user-friendly
- **Secure**: Shield imagery reinforces security

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, clear hierarchy
- **Body**: Readable, comfortable spacing
- **Financial Data**: Monospaced or tabular figures
- **CTAs**: Semibold, prominent

### Iconography
- **Style**: Line icons or filled
- **Weight**: Medium weight lines
- **Size**: Consistent sizing (24px, 32px, 48px)
- **Color**: Match brand palette

---

## 💰 Financial UI Patterns

### Color Meanings
- **Green (#3DBE3D)**: Income, savings, success
- **Red (#D32F2F)**: Expenses, debt, warnings
- **Blue (#1A44C8)**: Savings goals, investments
- **Amber (#FFB300)**: Caution, pending items

### Baby Steps Color Coding
Each step has a distinct color for quick recognition:
1. **Amber** - $1000 Emergency Fund
2. **Orange** - Debt Snowball
3. **Green** - 3-6 Months Expenses
4. **Blue** - 15% Retirement
5. **Purple** - College Fund
6. **Red** - Pay Off Home
7. **Gold** - Build Wealth & Give
